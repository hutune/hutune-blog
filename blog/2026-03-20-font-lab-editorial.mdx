## 1. Why this sample exists

Tech Briefing can mot bai test thuc te de danh gia typography khi noi dung dai va co nhieu lop heading.
This sample helps us validate long-form reading rhythm across Vietnamese and English in one article.

### 1.1 Reader promise

Ban co the doc theo 2 mode:

- Scan mode: chi doc heading + bullet.
- Deep mode: doc full paragraph voi context.

In both modes, typography should still feel stable and confident.

### 1.2 Expected behavior

Khoang cach dong can thoang de mat khong met khi doc 5-7 phut.
Headings need to remain distinct even after repeated sections and nested subsections.

## 2. Product narrative sample

### 2.1 Vietnamese block

Tech Briefing duoc xay dung de giai bai toan rat thuc te: tin cong nghe qua nhieu, nhung thoi gian cua nguoi doc thi co han.
Vi vay, moi bai duoc viet theo cau truc ngan gon, ro y va giu lien ket den bai goc de ai can dao sau co the di tiep ngay lap tuc.

### 2.2 English block

The publication is designed for high-signal updates, not content overload.
Each post prioritizes structure, context, and source traceability so readers can make fast decisions without losing depth.

## 3. Long-form readability section

### 3.1 Context paragraph

Khi doc cac bai tong hop, nguoi doc thuong can ba thu: mot, biet ngay chuyen gi dang xay ra; hai, hieu vi sao no quan trong; ba, co duong dan de kiem chung.
Neu typography khong giup nhin thay cau truc nay, bai viet se bi bien thanh mot khoi chu kho tieu hoa.

### 3.2 Additional context paragraph

This is exactly why heading hierarchy matters.
H2 should define the chapter, H3 should define the tactical question, and body text should complete the answer without visual noise.

### 3.3 Quote block

> Good editorial typography does not call attention to itself.
> It creates confidence, pace, and memory.

## 4. Structured content stress test

### 4.1 TL;DR style bullets

- Tin moi can duoc dat o dau bai.
- Muc "Why it matters" can co ngon ngu de hieu.
- Link nguon can de thay va de bam.

### 4.2 Mixed language bullets

- Short labels improve scanning.
- Long paragraphs improve trust.
- Balanced spacing improves retention.

### 4.3 Technical mini section

```txt
Signal > Noise
Clarity > Decoration
Context > Clickbait
```

## 5. Mobile behavior notes

### 5.1 Vietnamese

Tren mobile, line length ngan hon nen heading can ngan gon va paragraph can tach y ro rang.
Neu heading qua dai, nguoi doc se mat dau moc khi cuon nhanh.

### 5.2 English

On smaller screens, visual rhythm depends heavily on spacing.
If margins collapse too much, article comprehension drops even when content quality is high.

## 6. Conclusion

Option Editorial nay phu hop khi ban uu tien cam giac "newspaper + magazine", doc tin moi hang ngay va can muc do nghiem tuc cao.
