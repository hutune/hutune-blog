## 1. Muc tieu cua option nay

Neu ban muon blog co ban sac "Viet-friendly" hon, option nay la ung vien manh.
Dau tieng Viet hien ro, ky tu khong bi chen, va paragraph dai van giu duoc do mo mem.

### 1.1 Why it matters

Vietnamese typography fails quickly when the selected font lacks proper glyph balance.
This setup reduces that risk while preserving a professional editorial tone.

## 2. Bilingual reading section

### 2.1 Tieng Viet

Nguoi doc Viet thuong gap cac tu khoa ky thuat bang tieng Anh ngay trong mot cau tieng Viet.
Vi vay, bo font can dam bao do chuyen tiep giua hai ngon ngu khong bi "gay", dac biet o heading va bullet list.

### 2.2 English

A bilingual blog needs consistent rhythm across languages.
When typography is uneven, readers subconsciously trust the content less, even if the information is accurate.

## 3. Long section for scroll test

### 3.1 Section A

Trong thuc te van hanh, bai viet khong chi co mot doan ngan.
No thuong bao gom mo ta van de, boi canh he thong, du lieu tham chieu, va goc nhin trien khai.
Do do, viec test voi noi dung dai la bat buoc neu muon toi uu trai nghiem doc lau.

### 3.2 Section B

For English-heavy technical snippets, body text should remain neutral and crisp.
The goal is to prevent typography from becoming either too ornamental or too sterile.

### 3.3 Section C

Neu heading dep nhung body kho doc, tong the van that bai.
Neu body de doc nhung heading khong du noi bat, nguoi doc van kho scan.
Option nay co gang can bang hai diem do.

## 4. Header hierarchy stress

### 4.1 H2 as chapter marker

H2 nen du to de nguoi doc nhan biet "chuong moi" trong 1 cai nhin.

### 4.2 H3 as tactical marker

H3 dung de chia nho y, giup bai dai thanh cac khoi de xu ly.

#### 4.2.1 H4 as micro context

H4 nen dung tiet che, chi dung khi can phan cap chi tiet hon.
Qua nhieu H4 se lam bai nhin roi va mat tap trung.

## 5. Content pattern test

### 5.1 Value statement

Tech Briefing is not trying to replace original reporting.
It is designed to improve decision speed and reading efficiency.

### 5.2 Trust statement

Bai tong hop luon dan nguon goc.
Tom tat khong thay the bai chinh.
Nguoi doc co the kiem chung moi diem quan trong.

## 6. Ket luan

Option nay hop voi blog co audience Viet Nam, dong thoi van can doc hieu tot cac doan tieng Anh ky thuat.
