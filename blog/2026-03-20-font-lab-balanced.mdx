## 1. Positioning

Option nay phu hop khi ban muon blog vua "co chat noi dung" vua "co mui product".
No khong qua co dien nhu editorial thuần, nhung cung khong qua UI-like nhu dashboard.

### 1.1 Vietnamese lens

Cau van tieng Viet dai van giu nhiet do de doc on dinh, dac biet khi co nhieu menh de.
Khoang cach heading va body du thong thoang de tach y.

### 1.2 English lens

For English technical lines, Be Vietnam Pro still maintains clarity at smaller sizes.
This is useful when mixing summaries, metadata, and implementation notes in one flow.

## 2. Long section sample

### 2.1 Problem framing

Mot blog tong hop cong nghe khong the chi dep.
No can de doc, de scan, de tim lai thong tin, va de nhan ra cau truc khi nguoi doc quay lai lan 2.
Vi ly do do, typography phai ho tro tri nho thi giac.

### 2.2 System framing

The reading experience is a system, not a single style choice:

- Headings create navigation memory.
- Body text creates comprehension speed.
- Spacing creates cognitive breathing room.

### 2.3 Execution framing

Neu ban du dinh scale blog len nhieu bai moi ngay, bo font can on dinh trong nhieu context:

- short post
- long post
- list-heavy post
- source-heavy post
